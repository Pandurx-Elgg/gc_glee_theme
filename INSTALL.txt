/**
 * Installation
 */
 
1. Download 'glee' and 'glee_theme_draft_one'
2. Delete any previous installations of these plugins!
3. Copy both folders into your 'mod/' directory.
4. Put 'glee' below 'phloor' and 'glee_theme_draft_one' below 'glee'
5. Enable 'glee'
6. Enable 'glee_theme_draft_one'
7. Go to the settings page of 'glee' and choose your color
